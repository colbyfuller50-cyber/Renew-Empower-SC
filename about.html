About Colby Fuller

Veteran. Public servant. Neighbor. Ready to serve South Carolina House District 52 in 2026.

Rooted in Service

Colby Fuller served in the United States Air Force—including a tour supporting Operation Inherent Resolve—where he learned the value of honor, integrity, and teamwork.

Back home, he’s focused on helping families and small businesses thrive, improving schools, and keeping our communities safe.

Core Priorities

  • Economic Development: Cut red tape, grow jobs, support entrepreneurs.
  • Education: Expand STEM and career & technical education.
  • Healthcare: Improve rural access and patient outcomes.
  • Infrastructure & Environment: Plan smart, build resiliently.
  • Community Safety: Back the badge and strengthen prevention.
